Things to Explore in New York City

  • Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island – Iconic landmarks and immigration history.
  • Central Park – Scenic walks, boating, and outdoor activities.
  • Times Square – Bright lights, Broadway theaters, and bustling streets.
  • Empire State Building – Panoramic city views from the observation deck.
  • Brooklyn Bridge – Walk or bike across this historic bridge.
  • The Metropolitan Museum of Art – World-class art collections.
  • Broadway Shows – Catch a musical or play in the Theater District.
  • One World Observatory & 9/11 Memorial – Stunning views and historical reflection.
